# Hook Patterns for Shorts

## 10 Proven Patterns

### 1. Contrarian
- "Rich people don't save first — they buy assets first."
- "The most productive people don't use to-do lists."

### 2. Question
- "Ever notice how the busiest people get the least done?"
- "Why do smart people make dumb money decisions?"

### 3. Story Opener
- "I tried deep work for 30 days. Here's what happened."
- "Last year I read 52 books. One changed everything."

### 4. Challenge
- "Most people get this completely wrong."
- "This one mistake keeps you stuck."

### 5. Promise
- "One habit that will change your life."
- "The trick that 10x'd my productivity."

### 6. Surprising Fact
- "90% of lottery winners go broke."
- "Your environment controls 70% of your behavior."

### 7. Direct Address
- "If you're reading books but nothing changes..."
- "This is for the overthinkers."

### 8. Before/After
- "I went from broke to building wealth. Here's the shift."

### 9. Myth Buster
- "Motivation is a myth. Here's what actually works."

### 10. Number/List
- "3 books that made me $100K."
- "The 1% rule that changes everything."

## Avoid

- "In today's video..."
- "Hey guys, welcome back..."
- "Let's dive in..."
