# Generic Phrases to Avoid

## Opening Phrases (Never Use)

- "In today's video..."
- "Hey guys, welcome back..."
- "Let's dive in..."
- "Without further ado..."
- "So I've been thinking..."
- "What's up everyone..."
- "Before we get started..."

## Filler Phrases (Remove)

- "You know what I mean"
- "So basically"
- "Kind of like"
- "Sort of"
- "I mean"
- "To be honest"
- "At the end of the day"
- "It is what it is"

## Weak Transitions (Replace)

| Weak | Better |
|------|--------|
| "Moving on..." | [Just move on, no announcement] |
| "Now let's talk about..." | "Here's the next piece..." |
| "Another thing is..." | "This leads to..." |
| "Also..." | [Integrate naturally] |

## CTA Clichés (Avoid)

- "Smash that like button"
- "Don't forget to subscribe"
- "Hit the bell"
- "Let me know in the comments"
- "Share with a friend"

## Better Alternatives

Instead of announcing what you'll do, just do it:
- ❌ "Let me tell you about..."
- ✅ [Start telling]

Instead of asking for engagement, earn it:
- ❌ "If you found this helpful, like and subscribe"
- ✅ "Follow for 1 idea/day"

Instead of filler, add value:
- ❌ "This is really interesting because..."
- ✅ [State the interesting thing directly]
